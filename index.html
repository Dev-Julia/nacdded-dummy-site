AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

1

To have a common forum for the purpose of sharing ideas on common problems with regards to the strengthening and upholding of the philosophy, aims, objectives and practice of Catholic Education in accordance with the Catholic Church Policy on Education in Nigeria.

2

To ensure sound formation of the whole person through Catholic Education.

3

To ensure central planning, coordination and supervision of all Catholic schools and colleges in the Dioceses.

4

To ensure central planning, coordination and supervision of all Catholic schools and colleges in the Dioceses.

5

To act with common voice in relation to civil authorities in the interest of Catholic Education in Nigeria.

6

To ensure adequate welfare for all levels of staff in Catholic Educational Institutions.
